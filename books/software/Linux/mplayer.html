mplayer

远程控制mplayer

export DISPLAY=:0
mkfifo ~/mplayer-control 
nohup mplayer -slave -input file=~/mplayer-control <moviefile>

控制指令说明，请参考https://www.mplayerhq.hu/DOCS/tech/slave.txt

#比如停止和播放
echo pause > ~/mplayer-control 
#全屏
echo vo_fullscreen > ~/mplayer-control
